What Is Tempoyak?

Tempoyak is a traditional Southeast Asian condiment made from fermented durian, commonly used in Malaysian and Indonesian cuisines. Known for its pungent aroma and tangy, savory flavor, tempoyak is created by fermenting the flesh of ripe durian with a small amount of salt over several days. It is typically used as a flavorful base in spicy dishes, stews, or sambals, especially when paired with fish or seafood. Rich in probiotics and deeply rooted in local culinary heritage, tempoyak is both a cultural staple and a bold taste experience for those who enjoy fermented foods.
